5 Children & Youth Safety (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Holy Hour War Room takes the safety of minors seriously. The following rules apply to all accounts flagged as belonging to users under 18:

  • ✅ Minors cannot be added to any group without explicit parent or guardian consent
  • ✅ Youth accounts cannot initiate contact with adult members outside their verified group
  • ✅ Chat access for youth members requires a leader or parent to enable it individually
  • ✅ Prayer requests and praise reports from youth accounts are posted anonymously
  • ✅ A keyword safety filter automatically blocks messages containing phone numbers, addresses, emails, and grooming language
  • ✅ All blocked messages are logged and reviewed by the app's admin
  • ✅ We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 — accounts for children under 13 must be created and managed by a parent or guardian

For parents: If you believe your child has created an account without your knowledge or if you want to request deletion of their data, email us at debeortiz@gmail.com. We will respond within 72 hours and delete the account within 14 days.

Parent Approval Email: When a leader invites a minor to a group, a parent approval email is sent to the email you provide. The minor cannot join until you approve via the link in that email.

6 Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile, prayer requests, messages, and fasting records are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Content you posted to group chats is anonymised (sender name replaced with "Deleted User")
  • Backup copies are removed within 90 days

To request account deletion, email debeortiz@gmail.com with subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email used to register.

7 Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • Authentication is managed by Supabase Auth (industry-standard JWT tokens)
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data and data from groups they belong to
  • Avatar files are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket

No security system is perfect. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to debeortiz@gmail.com.

9 Cookies & Analytics

The Holy Hour War Room mobile app does not use cookies or third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK).

The only local storage used is AsyncStorage (on mobile) and localStorage (on web), used exclusively to persist your login session between app launches.

10 International Users

Holy Hour War Room is available globally. User data is stored on Supabase servers in the United States (AWS us-east-1) and EU (Supabase Frankfurt cluster, depending on your selected region).

EU / UK users (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing is contract performance (operating the app you signed up for) and legitimate interest (safety moderation). You have the rights listed in Section 8.

California users (CCPA): We do not sell personal data. You have the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (not applicable here).

Brazil users (LGPD): We comply with Brazil's General Data Protection Law. Contact us for any data subject requests.
